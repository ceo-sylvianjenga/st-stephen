Golf Club

The beautifully maintained 9-hole layout, which tests all skill levels, was designed by Walter Hatch, an assistant of the famed Donald Ross, and later renovated by Geoffrey Cornish. Measuring 3,075 yards from the back tees and 2,983 yards from the forward tees, with holes ranging from 160 to 535 yards, the par-35 course includes multiple tees, elevated tees and greens, many strategic green-side bunkers, and greens that are famous for being fast and true.
